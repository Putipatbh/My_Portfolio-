You’ll explore various research methods and techniques, learn how to create a comprehensive research plan, analyze your findings, and present your insights to stakeholders.

through the process of transforming user research insights into actionable design ideas and creating wireframes that serve as the foundation for intuitive, user-friendly digital products.
